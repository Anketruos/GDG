carry on

key point in the movie

Plot: A TSA agent is blackmailed by a mysterious criminal to let a dangerous item through security, leading to a race against time to stop a potential terrorist attack on a Christmas Eve flight.

Cast : Taron Egerton as Ethan Kopek (the TSA agent), Jason Bateman as the villainous "Traveler", and Sofia Carson as Ethan's girlfriend, Nora.

Director : Jaume Collet-Serra, known for his suspenseful thrillers.

Genre : Action thriller, with elements of a "Die Hard"-style high-stakes scenario.

Critical Reception

General Overview : Reviews for "Carry On" emphasize its intense atmosphere and captivating performances, particularly by leads Egerton and Bateman. Critics commend the film's gripping plot, though a few have pointed out minor inconsistencies that detract from the overall experience.


Character Portrayals: A significant highlight in the film is Bateman's portrayal of a casually menacing villain, which marks a notable shift from his typical comedic roles. This departure has garnered both acclaim and intrigue from audiences and critics alike.


Comparative Analysis: The film draws comparisons to classic airport thrillers, praised for its confined setting and high-stakes scenarios. This alignment with genre conventions enhances its suspenseful narrative and keeps viewers on the edge of their seats.
